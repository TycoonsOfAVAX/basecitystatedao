Real Estate Web3 Simluation

Be part of the real estate boom on Base Network this OnChain Summer, collect buildings of different types (residential, resource industry or government) to generate resources and collect rent (via staking rewards).

Behind each building boom are many different particpants. Construction is only one aspect, someone has to ensure there are enough workers and resources available. In this simulation we will be using a network of ERC20 and ERC721 tokens to simulate a building boom experience.

  • Construction & Real Estate

    The city isn't going to build itself, participate in the real estate boom of Base by building different buildings from residential to resource bearing buildings and vote generating civic buildings.

  • Resource farming

Getting Started

Get a Wallet

All game assets are stored in player's crypto wallets in line with the crypto community's value of self custody. The recommended way to play is through PC with chrome browser extension wallet, Metamask.

Players can still interact with smart contract's through the wallet's browser function.

Download wallets

Transfer Crypto

You need ETH on Base Network for all transactions. This can be done through buying Ethereum on Coinbase and withdrawing your ETH to your browser wallet as a BASE network crypto.

Send your crypto to Base Network

If you have ETH on the Ethererum already, you can bridge them over to Base L2 network through different bridges..

Getting Around

1- Acquire workers via Uniswap or via special events

2- Commit workers to construct basic buildings NFTs (residence & resource)

3- Stake buildings NFTs and start producing resources/perks

4- Sell resources to other players or use to construct advanced buildings

5- Particpate in governance and leave your mark in Base City
